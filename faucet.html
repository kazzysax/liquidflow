Arc testnet faucet

Liquid Flow runs on the Arc testnet, where the native token is USDC. Grab some free test USDC below, then use it to try a real payment or activate a gateway — no real money involved.

1 · Get test USDC

Test USDC is dispensed by Circle's official faucet. Open it, choose the Arc testnet, paste your wallet address, and request tokens.

Open Circle faucet →

2 · Arc testnet network details

Add these to your wallet manually if the button above doesn't work.

Network nameArc Testnet Chain ID5042002 (0x4CEF52) RPC URLhttps://rpc.testnet.arc.network CurrencyUSDC (6 decimals)

3 · Use it on Liquid Flow

  1. Create a gateway in the Access Gateway wizard and pay the small activation fee in test USDC.
  2. Or spin up a test payment in the dashboard and pay the deposit address from your funded wallet.
  3. Liquid Flow's watcher confirms the deposit on-chain automatically — you'll see it flip to confirmed.
