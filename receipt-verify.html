Receipt details
How verification works. A genuine receipt must pass two independent checks: the signature (Liquid Flow signs every receipt; tampering with any field breaks it) and the on-chain proof (the receipt points to a real transaction you can open on the block explorer). This page checks the signature live in your browser. The on-chain match is confirmed by Liquid Flow's backend in production; always open the explorer link to confirm the amount and destination yourself for high-value disputes.
