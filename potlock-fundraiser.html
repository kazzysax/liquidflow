Our local library was damaged in the floods. We're raising funds to replace books, shelving, and reading equipment so the space can reopen for students this term.

Where the funds go

Every contribution is recorded on-chain and the running total is visible to everyone on this page. Funds support replacing the lost collection, repairing the reading room, and restocking learning materials for the new term.

Why it matters

The library serves hundreds of students and families each week. Reopening it quickly means classes and study groups can continue without interruption.
