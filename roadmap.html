Roadmap

Where Liquid Flow is going

Sequenced deliberately: prove it on testnet, harden and audit it, add intelligence, then launch on mainnet. Non-custodial at every step.

● Phase 1 — Live on testnet

The core, working

All three systems run today on public testnets, fully non-custodial.

  • Three systems: Integrate Payment, Potlock (private fundraising), and Payroll.
  • Multichain: Base, Arc, Solana and Sui — EVM and non-EVM behind one API.
  • Stealth privacy: one-time addresses on secp256k1 (EVM) and ed25519 (Solana/Sui).
  • USDC payroll: business-owned contracts with one-transaction batch pay cycles.
  • Merchant dashboard, HMAC webhooks, copy-proof receipts, live confirmation.
◆ Phase 2 — Security & Audit (the mainnet gate)

Independent verification before real funds

Nothing touches mainnet until this phase is complete. Auditing is not optional — it is the gate.

  • Cryptographic audit — independent review of both stealth schemes (secp256k1 + ed25519), the highest-risk components.
  • Smart-contract audit — every contract on every chain, including ERC-20 payroll and batch release.
  • Formal verification — machine-checked proof of the "no unauthorized fund movement" invariant.
  • Testnet bug bounty — public program before mainnet.
  • Legal & regulatory review — money transmission, AML/KYC, travel rule, and the privacy posture.
✦ Phase 3 — AI integration

Intelligence on the payment layer

AI extends what the platform can do — always inside the non-custodial boundary. AI can suggest, draft, monitor and time actions; it can never hold or move funds directly. AI features are audited like everything else.

  • Agentic payments (AI-agent commerce) — let autonomous AI agents pay and get paid over Liquid Flow's rails using scoped, non-custodial keys, so agents can transact without custody.
  • Dashboard copilot — natural-language operations: "pay every contractor 500 USDC on the 1st", "flag payments over 1 ETH not yet confirmed" — turned into API/contract calls you approve.
  • Anomaly & fraud detection — models watch payment and payout patterns and surface anomalies into the circuit-breaker / pause controls.
  • Reconciliation & reporting — auto-match on-chain settlements to invoices and payroll runs; generate statements and receipts.
  • Compliance assistant — drafts screening and travel-rule metadata for human review (human-in-the-loop, never auto-moving funds).
→ Phase 4 — Expansion

Broader reach

  • More chains, continuously — additional EVM and non-EVM networks.
  • Recurring payroll — set-and-forget cycles that roll and remind you to fund.
  • Stablecoin settlement everywhere — ERC-20/USDC across payments and fundraising, not just payroll.
  • Fiat on/off ramps — partner integrations for entry and exit.
★ Phase 5 — Mainnet

Launch, staged

  • Audited mainnet deployment with a gradual, monitored rollout.
  • Production key management — trigger/operator keys move to HSM/KMS.
  • Observability & incident response — monitoring, alerting, and the circuit breaker wired end-to-end.
⚠️ This roadmap describes intent and sequencing, not dates or commitments. Today everything is testnet and unaudited — see Security.
